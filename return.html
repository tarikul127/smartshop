Return Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Overview

At TRAZA, your satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we offer an easy and flexible return policy. Please read the terms below carefully to understand how returns and refunds are handled.

2. Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products should be unused, undamaged, and in their original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or receipt) must be included with the return.
  • Certain items like earphones or opened software are non-returnable due to hygiene and safety reasons.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@Traza.com. Our team will guide you through the return procedure.

  • Request a return authorization by providing your order ID.
  • Carefully pack your item in the original packaging.
  • Ship it to the return address provided by our support team.
  • Once we receive your product, it will be inspected and processed within 5–7 business days.

4. Refund Policy

Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7–10 business days. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable unless the product was defective or incorrect.

5. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange your item for the same product, contact us at support@Traza.com.

6. Need Help?

If you have any questions about our return policy, feel free to reach out to our 24/7 support line: 01608603125. 127tarek7@gmail.com.
